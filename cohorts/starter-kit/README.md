
# Economic Performance of Philippine Provinces

## Problem Statement
I want to answer: "Which Philippine provinces have the highest and lowest GDP growth, and which industries are the primary drivers of that growth?"

## Audience
This project is for local government policymakers in the Philippines who need to make data-driven decisions about economic development and resource allocation.

## KPI or Main Metric
The main metric I want to track is Gross Domestic Product (GDP), specifically the annual growth rate and the industry contribution to each province's total GDP.

## Likely Data Source
I will explore the Philipppine Statistical Authority's Provincial Product Accounts in OPENSTAT (https://openstat.psa.gov.ph/Database/Economic-Accounts/Provincial-Product-Accounts).

## Possible Final Dashboard
The dashboard should help policymakers identify which provinces are outperforming or underperforming economically, and highlight the specific industries driving performances. This will help them decide where to focus infrastructure investment and which sectors to promote in their respective region.
